Urgent Appeal to Authorities

This site presents survivor-controlled evidence for an independent, trauma-informed review by the following agencies. It is tracker-free and ad-free so investigators, journalists, and the public can verify materials without barriers.

  • NAPTIP (Nigeria): mandated to investigate and prosecute trafficking offenses and coordinate victim services nationwide.
  • EFCC (Nigeria): empowered to investigate financial crimes, asset diversion, and related fraud.
  • HSI (USA): Homeland Security Investigations leads cross-border trafficking cases.
  • FBI (USA): investigates labor and sex trafficking in the U.S.
